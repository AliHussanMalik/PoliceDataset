RECOMMENDED USES
----------------
✓ Time-series trend analysis (2000-2024)
✓ Correlation studies: Crime vs socioeconomic variables
✓ Comparative analysis: Violent vs Property crimes
✓ Decade-over-decade comparisons (2000s vs 2010s vs 2020s)
✓ Publication on Kaggle, GitHub, LinkedIn with full methodology disclosure

NOT RECOMMENDED
---------------
✗ Granular year-to-year comparisons for projected years
✗ Precise forecasting without additional verification
✗ Provincial/district-level analysis (use 2012-2017 provincial dataset for this)
